My name is Takeru Yamada. I am thirteen years old. I am a first-year student at Midori Junior High School. I live in Yokohama with my family. There are five people in my family: my father, my mother, my sister Emi, my grandmother, and me. My father works at a restaurant. He is a chef. My mother works at a bookstore near our house.

I love cooking very much. I started cooking when I was ten years old. My father taught me how to cook. My best dish is curry rice. I can also make omelets and sandwiches. I cook dinner for my family every Saturday. My grandmother always says my food is delicious. That makes me very happy.

At school, my favorite subject is home economics because we can cook in class. I am also in the cooking club. There are twelve members in our club. We practice cooking every Tuesday and Thursday after school. Our club teacher is Ms. Sato. She is very kind and teaches us many recipes.

Next month, we will have a school festival. Our cooking club will sell pancakes at the festival. We are going to make three kinds of pancakes: chocolate, strawberry, and banana. I am the leader of the pancake team. We practice making pancakes every week. I want everyone to enjoy our pancakes.

My dream is to become a chef like my father. I want to open my own restaurant someday. I will study hard and practice cooking every day to make my dream come true.
