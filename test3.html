My name is Mika Suzuki. I am twelve years old. I am a first-year student at Nishi Junior High School. Last summer, my family went on a trip to Okinawa. It was my first time to visit Okinawa. We stayed there for five days. We went by airplane from Osaka. The flight took about two hours.

On the first day, we went to a beautiful beach. The sea was very blue and clear. I swam in the sea with my younger brother, Ryo. He is nine years old. He was a little scared of the sea at first, but soon he enjoyed swimming very much. My parents sat under a big umbrella and watched us. My mother took many pictures of us.

On the second day, we visited a large aquarium. It was called Churaumi Aquarium. I saw many kinds of fish there. The biggest fish was a whale shark. It was very big and amazing. I also saw dolphins. They jumped high out of the water. Ryo liked the sea turtles the best. We bought some postcards at the gift shop.

On the third day, we ate traditional Okinawan food at a local restaurant. I tried goya champuru for the first time. It was a little bitter, but I liked it. My father ate Okinawa soba. He said it was the best soba he ever had. After lunch, we went to Shuri Castle. It was very old and beautiful. We learned about the history of Okinawa there.

I had a wonderful time in Okinawa. I want to go there again someday. This trip is my best summer memory. I wrote about it in my summer homework diary.
